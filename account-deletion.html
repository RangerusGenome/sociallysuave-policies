How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Socially Suave account and associated data, please email us at:

Request Account Deletion

Required Information

Please include in your email:

  • The email address associated with your Socially Suave account
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account information (name, email)
  • Your conversation history and suggestions
  • Your practice mode data
  • Your usage statistics
  • Any uploaded screenshots

Retention Period

Your data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving your request. Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Subscription Cancellation

Please note: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. To cancel your premium subscription, please do so through Google Play Store settings before requesting account deletion.
