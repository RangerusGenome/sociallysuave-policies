Privacy Policy for Socially Suave

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

Introduction

Socially Suave ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Account Information: When you create an account using Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture
  • Google account ID

Usage Data

Conversation Suggestions: We temporarily process conversation text you input to generate suggestions. This data is:

  • Processed through our secure API
  • Not permanently stored on our servers
  • Stored locally on your device for history purposes (up to 30 days)

Feature Usage: We track:

  • Number of times you use each feature (conversation suggestions, ice breakers, practice mode, conversation analyzer)
  • Daily usage limits for free tier users

Images: When you upload screenshots for conversation analysis:

  • Images are processed to extract text
  • Images are not permanently stored
  • Extracted text is processed according to our data handling practices

Technical Data

  • Device information
  • App version
  • Operating system version
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  1. Provide Services: Generate conversation suggestions, ice breakers, and practice scenarios
  2. Authentication: Verify your identity using Google Sign-In
  3. Usage Tracking: Monitor feature usage for free tier limits
  4. Improve Services: Analyze app usage to enhance user experience
  5. Premium Features: Manage subscription status and access to premium features

Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage

  • User authentication data is stored in Firebase Authentication
  • Usage statistics are stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Subscription data is managed through Google Play Billing

Local Storage

  • Conversation suggestion history is stored locally on your device
  • History is automatically deleted after 30 days
  • You can manually delete history at any time

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Firebase security rules to protect user data
  • Secure authentication through Google Sign-In
  • No storage of sensitive conversation content on our servers

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Authentication, database, analytics
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Google Play Services

  • Purpose: Google Sign-In, in-app purchases
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Language Processing Services

  • Purpose: Generate conversation suggestions and analysis
  • Data Shared: Conversation text you provide (temporarily, for processing only)
  • Privacy Policy: Service Provider Privacy Policy

Vercel (Hosting)

  • Purpose: Backend API hosting
  • Privacy Policy: Vercel Privacy Policy

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Statistics: Retained for 90 days
  • Conversation History: Retained locally on your device for up to 30 days
  • Processed Text: Deleted immediately after processing

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  2. Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  3. Correction: Update incorrect personal information
  4. Opt-Out: Disable certain data collection features
  5. Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
